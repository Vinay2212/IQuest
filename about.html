            IQuest – Institute of Quality Engineering and Software Testing – is an institution for creating “Job Ready Professionals” through their industry oriented training and coaching programs on relevant technologies with Attitude Building Coaching Programs.

            The motto of iQuest is to provide high quality job oriented with industry relevance skills and personality development, emotional intelligence, assertiveness and other soft skills. Training and Coaching Programs for students of BE,BTech,MTech,ME, MCA, BSc, BCom, BBM, BCA and MBA so as to enable and empower them to be ready for jobs on the INTERNATIONAL PLATFORM. In our intense training program, the focus will be towards the overall development of candidates on Technologies, aptitude skills development, personality development, introduction and usage of the latest tools and techniques that are available and trending in the Industry.

            In iQuest we will be doing Competency Mapping / Pre-Assessment (SWOT Analysis). The batch size will be restricted to 25 participants for individual attention and course material will be provided. Role-play with video shoot, play back and re-visits for reinforcement are part of the program. A dedicated trainer will be assigned for the contract period. In IQuest, we have a host of visiting faculty to cover different subjects apart from special faculty for specific modules. The course would also provide coaching and mentoring in technical skills, soft skills and domain skills.

            One of the unique and distinguishing features of iQuest is that it prepares students to clear the Employability Assessment Test (EAT) conducted by NASSCOM and AMCAT by virtue of which the participants may not be required to appear for the written test of the employing company. Another key distinguishing feature of iQuest is the preparation it provides for the foundation level certification offered by the iSTQB, AWS, Oracle, IBM and other internationally recognized certification for graduate candidates who want to take up IT career as a profession. Participants will also be prepared to face different selection procedures like interviews, group discussion etc. iQuest will also be helping participants secure suitable jobs through its placement services.

            We also have tie ups with several colleges to take these programs to students and equip them with the right skills to keep pace with changing industry needs, thereby enhancing the placements of the college. Many industry leaders, academicians and graduates alike have lauded this initiative and recognised this is as the utmost need-of-the hour to balance the supply and demand of the IT sector.

            IQuest provides 100% Job Assistance and strives for continuous development for the success of Candidates who enrol for its Training Program.
